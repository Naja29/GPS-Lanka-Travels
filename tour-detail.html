Tour Overview

Kandy is a large city in central Sri Lanka. It's set on a plateau surrounded by mountains, which are home to tea and rubber plantations. Kandy is the cultural capital and last royal capital of the Sri Lankan Kings. The city is also the location of the Temple of the Tooth - one of Buddhism's most sacred shrines that houses the relic of the tooth of the Buddha.

This full-day tour takes you through the best of Kandy — from the iconic Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage to the serene Royal Botanical Gardens, the spice garden, a traditional Kandyan dance show, and finally the magnificent Temple of the Tooth Relic at sunset.

Tour Itinerary

1

Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage

Begin your journey at Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage — home to the world's largest herd of captive elephants. Watch baby elephants being bottle-fed and enjoy the famous elephant bathing ritual in the Maha Oya River. A truly heartwarming start to the day.

2

Kandy Spice & Herbal Garden

Visit a traditional spice garden and discover the incredible variety of Sri Lankan spices and medicinal herbs — cinnamon, cardamom, pepper, cloves and more. Expert guides explain the uses of each plant and traditional Ayurvedic remedies.

3

Royal Botanical Gardens - Peradeniya

Stroll through one of Asia's finest botanical gardens, home to over 4,000 plant species across 147 acres. Highlights include the Giant Javan Fig tree, the Orchid House, and the spectacular avenue of Royal Palms.

4

Kandy City & Local Market

Explore Kandy's vibrant city center and the bustling local market. Pick up unique souvenirs - handloom textiles, silver jewellery, carved wooden crafts, and Ceylon tea. A wonderful opportunity to experience everyday Sri Lankan life up close.

5

Traditional Kandyan Cultural Dance Show

Witness a spectacular performance of traditional Kandyan dance - one of Sri Lanka's most prized art forms. The show includes fire dance, plate dance, and the iconic Kandyan drumming that has been performed for centuries.

6

Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic

End your day with a visit to the magnificent Sri Dalada Maligawa - the Temple of the Tooth Relic. One of the most sacred Buddhist sites in the world, this golden-roofed temple houses the relic of the Buddha's tooth and is an unforgettable cultural experience at sunset.

Traveler Tips

What to Wear

Dress modestly when visiting temples - cover shoulders and knees. Bring comfortable walking shoes for the botanical gardens.

Best Time to Visit

Early morning departures are ideal to avoid heat and crowds at Pinnawala. The evening Puja ceremony at the temple is magical.

Photography

Photography is allowed at most sites. Bring extra memory cards - the elephant bathing and temple ceremonies are incredibly photogenic.

Food & Drinks

Lunch at a local Sri Lankan restaurant is included. Try the traditional rice and curry - a must-have authentic experience.
